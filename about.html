Our Story

Florida's Commercial & Industrial Fence Experts

Good Neighbor Fence Company is based in Tampa Bay and serves the commercial, industrial, and government fence market throughout the State of Florida. We have over 60 years of combined experience in the fencing profession and offer quality products, competitive pricing, and expert installation.

We're a family-owned firm with the capability to perform large projects statewide. Licensed, insured, and able to bond large projects. SBE certified with Hillsborough County, City of Tampa, Port Tampa Bay, and Pinellas County.

Our Services

What We Do

Good Neighbor Fence Company provides comprehensive fencing services across three core areas:

  • Temporary Fencing — Panel fencing, pounded post, barricades for construction and events
  • Permanent Fencing — Chain link, ornamental, wood, PVC, and specialty fencing
  • Gates & Access Control — Automated gates, keypads, card readers, and security systems

We handle projects of all sizes — from a single residential fence to multi-mile perimeter installations for solar farms and military bases.
